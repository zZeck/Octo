HPCVBBS
Hewlett Packard Corvallis Bulletin Board System, a dial-up system allowing users to discuss HP calculators and download HP48 software.
This was later available by telnet over the Internet, and has since shut down in favor of a web site only (and the BBS is now only Lynx pointed to the HP calculator website).
